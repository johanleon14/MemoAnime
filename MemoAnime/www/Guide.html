Guide

MemoSpace is a memory game that helps the brain practice short-term memory. The goal of the game is to try to find equal cards in a series of cards with different figures in each of them, which are even that is each drawomg os repeated.

Instructions

1- At the beginning of the game, all the cards are face down

2- The game player flips 2 cards

Case 1: The cards are different, the system turns them around

Case 2: The cards are the same, the system leaves them face up because the player has made a combination

3- Step 2 is repeated until there are no more cards face down on the table

4- Win the game by completing all the pairs

For all public

It is a game designed for the whole family, but as you can see practically anyone can take advantage of the memo from those who only seek to entertain themselves, to those who need to study for some test or who want to improve their mental capacity. It is a form healthy and fun entertainment that has remained in effect for a long time and is likely to continue that way in the future
